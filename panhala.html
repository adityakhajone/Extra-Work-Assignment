Panhala: The Magnificent Waterfall


One of the most popular hill stations of Maharashtra, Panhala is a beautiful place that comes alive during the monsoon season making it one of the best places to visit in monsoon in Maharashtra. The highlight of the place is the Panhala Fort and the magnificent waterfall. The fort is a reminder of the time when King Shivaji used to rule the area and is also the largest fort in the Deccan region. The fort still houses the temple that was installed by King Shivaji which speaks of nothing but the achievements of the King.

How To Reach:

Take a bus or train to Kolhapur, and hire a private cab to the destination



Ideal duration:

3 to 4 days



Things to do:

Trekking, visit the Panhala fort

Places To Stay:

Serenity Resort Panhala, Radhanand Holiday Home, Magadh, Studio Villas, Panhala Guest House



Places To Visit:

Panhala Fort, Sajja Kothi, Teen Darwaja, Shree Jotiba Devasthan



Best Time To Visit:

October to December
