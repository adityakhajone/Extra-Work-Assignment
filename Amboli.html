Amboli: Get The Soothing Vibes Of Hills


One of the most serene hill stations of Maharashtra, Amboli is known for its soothing vibes having a pleasant weather all throughout the year. Probably this why this place is considered as a famous for honeymoon destinations near Mumbai. It is the last hill station before the border of Goa starts and is supposedly a less explored place. So, if you’re in luck, you won’t find many people at this place and you can go touring all by yourself in this gorgeous land.



How To Reach:

Goa is the nearest airport.



Ideal duration:

1 to 2 days



Things to do:

Trekking at Durg Dhakoba and visiting Amboli falls, Nangarta falls and Madhavgad fort

Places To Stay:

Dark Forest Retreat, Garawa Resort, Vamoose Sailee, Vamoose Green Valley, Amboli Hill Resort



Places To Visit:

Amboli Ghat Waterfall, Amboli Sunset Point, Mahadev Gad, Shirgaonkar Point



Best Time To Visit:

Jun-Sep
