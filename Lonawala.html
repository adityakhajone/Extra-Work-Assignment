Lonavala: Explore The Scenic Beauty


Among the most celebrated hill stations near Mumbai and Pune, Lonavala hill station is a trekker’s paradise. With many waterfalls, caves, trekking and hiking points, it has become a favourite destination of adventure enthusiasts and nature lovers. And being a part of Sahyadri mountains, it offers some magnificent vistas. There are many picnic spots near Lonavala that are must visit when visiting this beautiful place.



How To Reach:

Lohegaon Airport in Pune is the nearest airport and Lonavala has its own railhead. People also prefer to drive from Mumbai and Pune.

Ideal duration:

1-2 days



Things to do:

Explore Rajmachi Wildlife Sanctuary, Amrutanjan Point and Bhaja Caves. Trek up to Koregad Fort, Tiger’s Leap, Duke’s Nose, and Kondane Caves. You can also go for boating in Lonavala Lake.



Places To Stay:

Hilton Shillim Estate Retreat and Spa, Meritas Adore Resort, Hotel Ferreira Resort, The Dukes Retreat, EKO STAY



Places To Visit:

Bhushi Dam, Pawna Dam, Tungarli Lake



Best Time To Visit:

Throughout the year
