Chikhaldara:Kashmir Of Vidarbha


Located in Amravati, Chikhaldara is one of the best hill stations in Maharashtra that offers majestic views and excellent wildlife. Chikhaldara is the only place in the region that produces coffee making it famous amongst coffee lovers. The rich smell of the coffee engulfs the place and will put you in a trance as soon as you set foot in the area. The magnificent view of the hills coupled with the glory of the Semadoh lake is the highlight of this place.

How To Reach:

From Mumbai and Pune you can take a train to Amravati. From there a taxi can be hired to reach the destination.



Ideal duration:

1 to 2 days



Things to do:

sightseeing, visit forts, Melghat Tiger Reserve
