Sawantwadi: A Peaceful Getaway


Tucked between Sahyadri hills on one side and coastal road on the other, Sawantwadi is known for peaceful environs, serene lakes, vintage temples, plush green forests, and sumptuous authentic Konkani food. Except exploring Sawantwadi Palace and Moti Talao Lake, the place has nothing much to do. Sawantwadi is basically for those in search for a tranquil place filled with natural wonders.

How To Reach:

The nearest airport is at Dabolim, Goa. The place has its own railhead named Sawantwadi road.



Ideal duration:

2 to 3 days
