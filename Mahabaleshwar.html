Mahabaleshwar


Mahabaleshwar is counted as the best hill stations in Maharashtra. The intoxicating aromas of strawberries and misty mornings allure tourists from Pune, Mumbai, and other nearby cities. It’s among the most gorgeous hill stations near Pune, for those who want to experience the romantic monsoon and winter of Mahabaleshwar. This Maharashtra hill station is also a photographer’s paradise. The spectacular viewpoints like Kate’s Point and Elephant Point, Venna Lake, Chinaman waterfalls and Connaught peak are some of the major attractions here. One can also see the origin point of River Krishna at Mahadev Temple, located in old Mahabaleshwar.

How To Reach:

Pune is the nearest airport, located 120 kms away and Wathar, which is 60 kms away is the closest railway station. The drive from Mumbai and Pune to Mahabaleshwar is scenic, taking you through the breathtaking Western Ghats.



Ideal duration:

3 to 4 days



Things to do:

Mountain biking, rock climbing, nature trails, horse riding, and relishing sunrise from Wilson Point.

Places To Stay:

Le Méridien Mahabaleshwar Resort & Spa, Gavson Palace, Sagar Villa Panchgani, Evershine Keys Resort, JK Motels



Places To Visit:

Venna Lake, Mini Kashmir, Lord Mahabaleshwar Temple



Best Time To Visit:

December – February
